A couple of things
to get you started
The web based wallet application is a quick way to send and receive Decred. To participate in stake mining and voting, take a look at our standalone wallets.
Local and secure
All of your wallet data is kept in the local storage of your browser. For safest usage, a web wallet should not be created on a publicly accessible device. It's possible to delete your web wallet, even unintentionally, by clearing the browser cache.
The key to your wallet
Your seed phrase is an encrypted version of your key. It's the only way to access your wallet and funds. Keep it in a safe place to prevent the loss of your funds. The same key can be used later to recreate your wallet in other Decred clients.
